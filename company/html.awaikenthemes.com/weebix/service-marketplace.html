E-Commerce Marketplace Management

We manage your seller accounts and product listings on Amazon, Walmart, eBay, and Houzz — from product setup to advertising and daily operations. Our marketplace management services help you maximize sales and grow your presence across multiple platforms.

Our services include seller account setup and optimization, product listing creation with keyword optimization, A+ Content and Enhanced Brand Content for Amazon, and PPC advertising management including Sponsored Products and Brands. We handle inventory management and restocking, pricing strategy and Buy Box optimization, and product photography coordination.

We also provide review and feedback management, multi-channel inventory sync, returns and customer service handling, and monthly sales reporting with detailed analytics. Whether you're just starting on a marketplace or looking to scale your existing operations, our team has the expertise to drive results.
