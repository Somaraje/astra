5 Best Payment Gateway Options for Your Online Store in 2026

  • 22 February 2026
  • E-Commerce
  • Asthra Digital Solutions Team

Choosing the right payment gateway is one of the most critical decisions for any online store. The payment experience directly impacts your conversion rates, customer trust, and bottom line. A complicated or untrustworthy checkout process can cause customers to abandon their carts, while a smooth, secure payment flow builds confidence and encourages repeat purchases. Here are the 5 best payment gateway options for your online store in 2026.

1. Razorpay — Best for Indian Businesses

Razorpay has become the go-to payment gateway for Indian businesses, and for good reason. It supports over 100 payment methods including UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, wallets, and EMI options. The onboarding process is fast and straightforward, with no setup fees. Razorpay charges a flat 2% transaction fee, making it cost-effective for businesses of all sizes. Its dashboard provides detailed analytics, and it integrates seamlessly with WooCommerce, Shopify, and custom websites.

2. PayPal — Best for International Transactions

PayPal remains one of the most trusted payment brands globally, with over 400 million active accounts. If you sell internationally, PayPal is almost essential — many overseas customers prefer PayPal because of its buyer protection policies. PayPal supports 25 currencies and is available in over 200 countries. The main drawback is higher fees for international transactions (around 4.4% + fixed fee), but the trust factor and global reach make it worthwhile for cross-border sales.

3. Stripe — Best for Developers and Custom Solutions

Stripe is the payment gateway of choice for tech-savvy businesses and developers. Its API is beautifully designed, well-documented, and incredibly flexible. Stripe supports subscriptions, one-time payments, marketplace payments, and even custom checkout experiences. With Stripe, you can build exactly the payment flow you want. It charges 2.9% + 30 cents per transaction for domestic cards, and supports over 135 currencies for international payments.

The right payment gateway doesn't just process transactions — it builds customer confidence. When customers trust your checkout, they buy more and come back more often.

4. Cashfree Payments — Best for Fast Settlements

Cashfree Payments is gaining popularity in India thanks to its instant settlement feature — you can receive funds in your bank account within 15 minutes of a successful payment. It supports UPI, cards, net banking, wallets, and Pay Later options. Cashfree is particularly good for businesses that need fast cash flow. It also offers a powerful payout system for sending money to vendors, employees, or customers instantly.

5. CCAvenue — Best for Maximum Payment Options

CCAvenue is one of India's oldest and most comprehensive payment gateways, supporting over 200 payment options including international cards, domestic cards, net banking from 58+ banks, UPI, mobile wallets, and EMI from all major banks. If offering the maximum number of payment options is your priority, CCAvenue is hard to beat. It's especially popular with large e-commerce websites and enterprise clients.

  • Transaction fees — compare per-transaction costs carefully, as small differences add up significantly at scale.
  • Settlement time — how quickly you receive your money matters for cash flow management.
  • Security standards — ensure the gateway is PCI DSS compliant and supports 3D Secure authentication.
  • Integration ease — check for WooCommerce/Shopify plugins or well-documented APIs for custom integration.
  • Customer support — choose a gateway with responsive support, especially during your launch phase.
